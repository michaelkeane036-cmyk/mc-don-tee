About Me

Who is MC Don Tee?

Olaniyi Tabi, popularly known as MC Don Tee, is a highly experienced host, compere, and TV show presenter with over 20 years in the entertainment industry. A true professional in his craft, he is known for his confidence, energy, and ability to connect effortlessly with any audience.

As a versatile and knowledgeable entertainer, MC Don Tee has built a strong reputation for excellence, delivering outstanding performances at events of all kinds. He is also a seasoned traveling MC who has traveled around the world, bringing life, class, and excitement to stages across different countries and cultures.

With his vast experience and dynamic presence, MC Don Tee continues to stand out as a trusted and respected voice in hosting and presentation.
